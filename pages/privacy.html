Privacy Policy

Last updated:

At QRForms, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you register or use the Service, we collect:

  • Personal info: Name, email address, mobile number, date of birth
  • Business info: Company name, address, city, state, PIN code, website, company size
  • Account credentials: Password (stored as encrypted hash, never in plain text)
  • Form content: Forms you create, branding details (logo, contact info)
  • Submission data: Responses collected from your form respondents

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and language preference
  • Pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies for session management

1.3 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Razorpay. We do NOT store your card details. We only receive transaction status and reference IDs from Razorpay.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Generate QR codes and store form submissions
  • Communicate with you (account updates, support, important notices)
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

3. How We Share Information

We may share your information with:

  • Razorpay (payment processor): To process your payments securely
  • Cloud infrastructure providers: To host and store data securely
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

All third-party providers are required to maintain confidentiality and security of your data.

4. Form Submission Data

When customers fill out forms you created:

  • The submitted data belongs to YOU (the form owner)
  • We store it solely to display it in your dashboard and allow Excel export
  • We do not use the submission data for any other purpose
  • You are responsible for obtaining consent from respondents and complying with applicable data laws

5. Data Storage and Security

We take security seriously:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption
  • Data in transit is protected by HTTPS/TLS
  • Data at rest is stored in encrypted cloud databases
  • Access to systems is restricted and audited
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

While we use industry-standard practices, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account closure:

  • Account data is retained for 90 days, after which it may be permanently deleted
  • You can request immediate deletion by emailing us
  • Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations

7. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023 Compliance)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent: For any data processing based on consent
  • Grievance: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer (contact below)

To exercise these rights, email us at support@qrforms.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Keeping you logged in (session cookies)
  • Remembering your language preference
  • Analytics to improve the platform

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers located in India or other countries. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers, which are conducted with appropriate safeguards.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review the privacy policies of any sites you visit.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or platform notification. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.

13. Grievance Officer

If you have concerns about your data privacy, you can contact our Grievance Officer:

Email: support@qrforms.com
Address: -

We aim to respond to all grievances within 30 days.

14. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, email us at support@qrforms.com.
