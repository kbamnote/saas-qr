About QRForms

Building tools that make data collection effortless

Our Mission

We believe collecting customer data shouldn't require expensive software, complex setup, or technical skills. Our mission is to give every business — from a single shop owner to a large enterprise — a simple, beautiful way to collect responses through QR codes.

What We Do

Our platform lets you:

  • Build custom forms with any fields you need
  • Generate branded QR codes that work on any phone
  • Collect responses automatically into a clean dashboard
  • Export everything to Excel with one click
  • Create different QR codes for different events or campaigns

Who We Serve

Businesses across India trust us for:

  • Restaurants & Cafes — collecting customer feedback at tables
  • Retail Stores — gathering customer details for loyalty programs
  • Event Organizers — managing registrations and feedback
  • Educational Institutions — student information collection
  • Healthcare Clinics — patient intake forms
  • Service Providers — appointment requests and inquiries

Why Choose Us

  • Made in India: Built specifically for Indian businesses with INR pricing and Razorpay integration
  • No Technical Skills Needed: If you can use WhatsApp, you can use our platform
  • Affordable: Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Secure: Your data is encrypted and stored safely
  • Multilingual: Available in English, Hindi, and Marathi
